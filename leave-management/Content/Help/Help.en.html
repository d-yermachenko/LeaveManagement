In brief

This example allows you to simulate the leave management in your company. You can ask the administrator to create your company or create your own test company and then edit leave types and test employees for your company. After editing the lists of employees and leave types for your country and company, you are able to allocate leave to your test employees. After leave was allocated, opens possibility to make the leave requests. Then manager is able to accept or reject your leave request.

Creating test company

This DEMONSTRATION possibility allows to create the test company with 4 default fake leave types and with 3-8 fake employees. You need only the name of your test company and WORKING email address to receive all demo passwords. I suggest you to use temporary email, that you can instantly obtain from the sites like

After creatin the test company, you will receive the login data for your test employees. Then you can simulate the leave management on your company.

Using leave management.

Applying for leave

Click this action in menu and form will guide you for applying to live. You need to choose the type of vacations and give the period to leave. You can also type the comment to your validator.

In the bottom of the form there is statistics of your leaves allocations and request to give you an idea about sold of any leave type.

My (your) leaves

Information about past and current leave requests. It is possible to edit or remove the leave request before it was accepted. You can also remove your leave request in less than 3 (three) days before beginning.

Leave management

Reviewing requests

Once your employee posted his request, it appears in review section in “pending” state. You will also see the notification in your notification area. You can decide, if you can accept it or you must reject it. You can also delete it. Why – I don’t know, but you can. May be because you can.

Employee will see if his/her request was handled in notification area.

Allocating leaves

Allows allocate leave to your employees. There is two ways of leave allocation: Massive allocation and precise allocation

This part available only for Company administrators and HR managers

Massive allocation

Allocates leave of given type for all employees in the company. The default duration applied.

Precise allocation (Allocate leaves)

Allows you to choose the employee to allocate the leave. In same page you’re able to navigate to already allocated leaves to this employee and watch his details.

To allocate leave, click the button “allocate”. This action will navigate you to page, where you will choose leave type and duration to allocate.

Details

Allows to see all the allocations of all the employees.

Administration

Editing leave types

Creating, editing and removing leave types. All related allocations and requests will be updated in cascade. This part is available only for company administrators and HR managers.

Editing employees

Creating, editing and removing employees. All related allocations and requests will be updated in cascade. This part is available only for company administrators and HR managers.
